S-Trip

User Experience and Visual Designer

S-Trip (Student Trip) is a Canadian youth travel company which provides young people with options for holidays & vacations. The site was to appeal to the parents as well as teens, as are the decision makers and were found to be high users of the site.

My role was to work closely with the client to develop a sitemap and wireframes. I then compiled research on appropriate tone of voice for the audience before creating the design concepts.

User Experience and Visual Designer

My role at Skype was interaction and visual design for Skype Manager. Skype Manager is a web application allows businesses to log in, allocate Skype Credit and features to their employees as well as monitor their usage and view historical reports.

I worked on several projects and updates for Skype Manager's future releases, one project being an update to Skype Connect.
